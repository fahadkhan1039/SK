Vision and Values

Our History

Archipix was founded in 1995 in California, USA. The company was established by Louis Skidmore and Nathaniel Owings, and later joined by John O. Merrill.

The company has designed the John Hancock Center, One World Trade Center and Burj Khalifa, which is currently the tallest building in the world. Throughout its history, Archipix has been recognized for its innovative designs and commitment to sustainability.

Archipix Founding

1995

The company is founded by John Smith, an experienced architect with a passion for sustainable design.

Completing The First Project

1996 - 1998

The company completes its first major project, a LEED-certified office building that sets a new standard.

Expanding Services

2001

The company expands its services to include interior design, and hires its first interior designer.
